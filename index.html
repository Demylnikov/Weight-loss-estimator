How does it work?

The main principle of losing weight is CICO (Calories In, Calories Out). In other words, in order to lose weight you have to burn more calories than you intake.

This app can calculate and visualize your weight loss progress based on some parameters. The calculation itself is based on Harris-Benedict equations (revies by Mifflin and St. Jeor) and is fairly accurate.
